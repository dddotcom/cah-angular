1. The Card Czar controls the game by reading the question or fill-in-the-blanks phrase on the black card out loud.
  2. The other players answer the question or fill in the blanks by each selecting one white card (or however many required by the black card), which will display face down.
  3. The Card Czar then picks the funniest answer, and whoever submitted it gets one "Awesome Point".
  4. After the round, a new player becomes the Card Czar, and the cycle will begin again.
